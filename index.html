FAQs – Getting Started with Syntheos

When do I get access?

Early testers will be invited starting May 2025. Priority goes to the first 200 users on the waitlist.

Do I need a wallet?

Yes. You will need a MetaMask (or any EVM-compatible) wallet to access our testnet. We'll guide you step-by-step — no prior DeFi experience required.

Is there a fee?

No. Testing Syntheos is 100% free. You’ll use testnet stablecoins, not real money.
